Omnija Kronologio
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2 reference points X and Y;
Other acronyms:

- J: ***Jaroj***    | years
- M: ***Monatoj***  | Months
- S: ***Semajnoj*** | Weeks
- T: ***Tagoj***    | Days
  - Here we assume one year is 13 months exactly, except leap year;
    and each months has 4 weeks exactly:
    $$1J = 13M = 52S = 364T$$
- h: ***Horoj***    | Hours
- m: ***Minutoj***  | Minutes
- s: ***Sekundoj*** | Seconds
